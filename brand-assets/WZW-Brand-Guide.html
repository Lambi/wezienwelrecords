01

Brand Essence

A living symbiosis of sound and friendship.

WeZienWel Records is an independent house, deep, jazz and jam label founded in 2019 in Ghent — now spanning Amsterdam, Ghent, and Lisbon. It is more than a label; it is a current shaped by shared moments. Jamming is our language.

The brand is editorial, honest, and warm — typographically strong, never corporate. Everything communicates substance over style, with the red as the only moment of emotion.

Mission

Release music that matters to people who feel it. No genre gatekeeping, no ego — just frequency.

Personality

Warm · Editorial · Honest · Understated · Rooted · Alive

04

Logo Rules.

The wordmark is the logo — "WeZienWel" in EB Garamond 800 + "Records" in JetBrains Mono. The SVG logo file lives in /assets/logo.svg.

✓ Do

Use on Paper or Ink backgrounds only. Keep clear space equal to the height of the "W" on all sides. Use the red accent sparingly — one element at most.

✗ Don't

Don't place on busy photo backgrounds without an overlay. Don't stretch or distort. Don't change font families. Don't use the wordmark smaller than 120px wide.

✓ Do

Pair with mono labels like "AMS · GHE · LIS" or "EST. 2019" in Dust colour underneath for context and ground.

✗ Don't

Don't use Blue as a logo colour. Blue is reserved for rare interactive states only. Never apply drop shadows or effects to the wordmark.

05

Design Principles.

Borders over fills

Use 1.5px solid Ink borders to define space. Avoid decorative fills. The border IS the design.

One red rule

Maximum one red element per layout block — a bar, a dot, an italic word. Never two.

Mono for metadata

All catalog numbers, dates, locations, and technical info must be in JetBrains Mono, uppercase, with letter-spacing.

Italic for emotion

EB Garamond italic signals feeling — titles, quotes, the word "Wel" in the logo. Don't dilute it.

White space is structure

Generous padding, minimal elements. The blank space communicates confidence. Don't fill everything.

Paper background first

Default to Paper (#F2EEE5) backgrounds. Use Ink backgrounds for "Now Playing" / hero moments only.

06

Tone of Voice.

The label communicates like a person, not a brand. Warm, brief, and specific — never hype, never corporate.

Write this

"There Is A Place — new music by Cris Kai. Out today. Listen everywhere."

Not this

"🔥🔥 HUGE drop just landed!! Our incredible artist Cris Kai is BACK with an ABSOLUTE BANGER!!"

Write this

"A living symbiosis of sound and friendship. Est. 2019. Ghent → Amsterdam → Lisbon."

Not this

"WeZienWel Records is a leading independent house music label with a globally recognized roster of artists."
