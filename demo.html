Good to know

How we listen.

  • Send finished work. We prefer mastered or near-final tracks over rough sketches.
  • Private links only. Don't send us public releases — we're looking for unreleased material.
  • One project at a time. Pick the one track or EP you most want us to hear.
  • Turnaround. We listen to every submission but only reply if there's a fit. Give us 2–6 weeks.
  • Format. WAV or AIFF preferred for files. 320 kbps MP3 is fine to start.
  • The frequency. We release house, deep, jazz and live jam music — improvisation and groove over genre labels.
